Susan's Company — Admin Guide

A simple guide to using your admin panel

1. How to Login

1 Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, or any browser).
2 Go to your admin login page:
[your Vercel URL]/admin-login.html
3 Enter your email address and password.
4 Click the Login button.
Tip: Keep your login details safe. If you forget your password, contact the person who set up your website.

2. How to Add a Product

1 Click Products in the left menu.
2 Click the green + Add New Product button at the top.
3 Fill in the product details:
Name — What the product is called (e.g. "Modest Hijab Dress")
Price — The price in Kenyan Shillings (e.g. 2500)
Category — Choose "Clothes" or "Books"
Description — A short description of the product
WhatsApp Message — A message customers will see when they click Order (optional)
Discount Price — If the product is on sale, enter the sale price here (optional)
In Stock — Check this box if the product is available
4 Click Save Product.
5 Your product will appear on the shop page immediately.

3. How to Edit a Product

1 Go to the Products page.
2 Find the product you want to change in the list.
3 Click the gold Edit button next to that product.
4 Change whatever you need to (name, price, description, etc.).
5 Click Save Product to save your changes.

4. How to Delete a Product

1 Go to the Products page.
2 Find the product you want to remove.
3 Click the red Delete button.
4 A pop-up will ask "Are you sure?" — click OK to confirm.
Warning: Deleting a product cannot be undone. Make sure you really want to remove it before confirming.

5. How to See Customer Messages

1 Click Messages in the left menu.
2 You will see a list of messages from customers.
3 To reply to a customer, click the green Reply on WhatsApp button. This will open WhatsApp so you can send them a message.
4 When you have finished reading a message, click Mark as Read to mark it as done.
Tip: Unread messages are shown at the top. The yellow badge at the top of the page tells you how many unread messages you have.

6. How to Track Orders

1 Click Orders in the left menu.
2 You will see a list of all customer orders.
3 Each order has a status. You can change the status using the dropdown menu:
Pending — The customer has placed an order but you have not contacted them yet.
Contacted — You have reached out to the customer on WhatsApp.
Delivered — The order has been delivered to the customer.
Cancelled — The order was cancelled.
4 Click the dropdown and select the new status whenever the order changes.
Tip: The Dashboard page shows you a quick summary of all your orders — how many are pending, contacted, and delivered.

7. How to Logout

1 Click Logout in the left menu.
2 A pop-up will ask "Are you sure you want to logout?" — click Logout to confirm.
Tip: Always logout when you are finished, especially if you are using a shared or public computer.
